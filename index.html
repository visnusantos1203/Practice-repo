About Us Programs Speakers Events

A series of masterclasses and public sessions will be held in September 2020 under the program of Film Industry Conference (FIC). The FIC, first conducted by the FDCP in 2017, it aims to bring together international experts and stakeholders to share and discuss the latest trends, opportunities, platforms, and cooperation that Filipino producers and filmmakers can explore for the development, production, and distribution of their projects with the intent of crossing over beyond local borders.

As we shift our programs to an online platform this 2020, we are launching Film Industry Conference Online 2020 featuring our local and international partners with one goal to continue the synergy between international experts, professionals, and stakeholders even online. This will be a great opportunity to give a platform and discuss the best practices, challenges, opportunities, and new norms of the industry given our current situation in a global pandemic.

Atendees: Open to the public FOR PRE-REGISTRATION

The FIC Features:

  • Access All Eight(8) Public Sessions via zoom for Free
  • Masterclasses are recommended for film industry professionals and enthusiasts alike who would like to learn more about specific topics:
    • Master Class 1: Writing During Quarantine - Php. 800.00 / USD 16
    • Master Class 2: Filmmaker's Guide to Film Distribution - Php. 1,000.00 / USD 20
    • Master Class 3: Financing Your Film and Getting a Co-Production on a Lockdown - Php. 800.00 / USD 16
    • Master CLass 4: Editing During Quarantine - Php. 1,000.00 / USD 20
    • Master Class 5: Pitching Your Project Virtually - Php. 800.00 / USD 16
    • Master Class 6: The Success of Marketing Distribution of Genre Films - Php. 800.00/ USD 16
  • Premium Access to All Masterclasses at Php. 4,000.00 / USD 80
